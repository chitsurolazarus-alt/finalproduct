About Candies Cleans

Introducing innovative and life changing services from Candies Cleans, our brand new maid service. Our committed maids are trained to bring you that spotless touch to every nook and cranny and to every inch of your space. From maid training services to giving you spotless space.

Being an African woman, Candies realized there are opportunities to avail herself of in the Black Economic Empowerment structures of the country. She is a determined person who has made a success of her business.

What inspired me to impact on this dynamic duo was not only to create a business that delivers spotless spaces or a perfect clean, but one that uplifts my community.

We offer maids training services. Invest in your maids growth with our specialised training. Give your maids the gift of professional development with our training services. Transform your home with trained and confident household helpers.

Candies Cleans provides contract cleaning and specialized cleaning services, focussing on the variety of industries and companies around the world. Other services include the provision of hygiene services, pest control, once-off cleans, waste management and training & learnerships.

We are located all over South Africa.
